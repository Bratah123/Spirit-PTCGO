Reward Ladder

Players earn ladder points per Versus match (win > loss). When their season points cross a milestone, its rewards are granted automatically — tokens go to the wallet, cards and products go to the collection. The ladder is split into tiers: the in-game screen shows one tier at a time and auto-advances to the tier holding the player's next milestone. Click a milestone's number to edit its point threshold. The game renders up to 3 reward arts per milestone (cards fan out, packs show a single tile); a milestone with several rewards must include at least one card or product.

Tournaments (in-game Events tab)

Tournaments appear on the client's Events screen while entries are open. Players pay the entry fee to queue; when the queue fills to the bracket size, a live single-elimination bracket starts and pays the prize rows by final PLACE (1 = champion). The win/loss caps and leaderboard below additionally drive the (currently UI-less) async run system.
